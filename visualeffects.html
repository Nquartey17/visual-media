Invert

Invert changes all the colors to be opposite of what they normally would be. Another name for this effect is called negative.

Black and White

A self explanitory effect. Makes video footage look like it's from a time before color footage was produced

Newblue Earthquake - Hand Held and Sapphire - Cartoon

Newblue and Sapphire are some plugin effects that can be purchased for Sony Vegas, Adobe Premiere and Adobe After Effects to name a few. They offer a variety of effects that weren't originally included within the program. Notice how the screen shakes. That is the Newblue Earthquake effect while the strange looking style is the Sapphire Cartoon plugin.

Chroma Keyer: Green Screen

This effect allows editors to get green screen footage and place it over the original footage. In Adobe After Effects, you can create your own green screens or remove video footage from another video to create the green screen.

This zoom effect is one of many that can be downloaded online. Before you think about making your own green screen effects, check online to see if somebody has already created the effect you're looking for. Making green screens can be complicated and time consuming.
